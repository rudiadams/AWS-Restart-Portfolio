# Fundamentals of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

## Overview

This category encompasses my foundational training in artificial intelligence and machine learning concepts, from core terminology to practical AWS implementations. I explored the distinctions between AI, ML, deep learning, and generative AI, while gaining hands-on experience with AWS's AI-powered services for real-world applications.

## Knowledge I Have Obtained

| Service/Concept | Description |
|---|---|
| Amazon Bedrock | I learned to leverage foundation models through a managed service, understanding how to access and interact with large language models, diffusion models, and multimodal models without managing infrastructure. |
| Amazon Comprehend | I demonstrated how to extract meaningful insights from unstructured text data using natural language processing, including sentiment analysis and entity recognition capabilities. |
| Amazon Polly | I configured text-to-speech synthesis to generate lifelike audio from text content, understanding how to apply AI for accessibility and content delivery. |

## Services/Tools I Worked With

* **Amazon Bedrock** — A fully managed service providing access to foundation models from leading AI companies, enabling me to build generative AI applications without managing underlying infrastructure.
* **Amazon Comprehend** — An NLP service that extracts insights from unstructured text through sentiment analysis, entity recognition, and key phrase detection.
* **Amazon Polly** — A text-to-speech service that converts written content into lifelike audio, supporting multiple languages and voice options.

## Key Tasks I Performed

* Distinguished between AI, machine learning, deep learning, and generative AI—understanding the relationships and specific use cases for each.
* Defined and applied foundational ML terminology including supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning paradigms.
* Analyzed the types of data required to train AI models and their impact on model performance and bias.
* Explored neural network architectures and how they power modern deep learning applications.
* Mapped the foundation model (FM) lifecycle from pre-training through fine-tuning and deployment.
* Evaluated the three primary categories of foundation models: large language models, diffusion models, and multimodal models.
* Assessed AWS AI services and identified appropriate use cases for enterprise solutions.

## What I Demonstrated

* A solid understanding of AI and ML fundamentals, capable of explaining complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
* Knowledge of foundation models and their practical capabilities in generating text, images, and multimodal content.
* Hands-on proficiency with AWS managed AI services, understanding how to leverage them without building models from scratch.
* The ability to evaluate AI/ML approaches for business problems and select the right AWS service for the job.
* Awareness of responsible AI principles and the considerations involved in deploying foundation models at scale.

# Auto-Healing and Scaling Applications

## Overview
My exploration of application resilience and elasticity — analyzing auto-healing and scaling capabilities provided by Auto Scaling groups, configuring scaling with defined resource boundaries, and designing scaling strategies that respond to time-based and performance events.

---

## Services I Worked With

- **Amazon EC2 Auto Scaling** — service that automatically adjusts EC2 instance capacity to maintain performance and manage costs based on demand
- **Amazon CloudWatch** — monitoring service used to track metrics and trigger scaling actions based on defined thresholds
- **Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud (EC2)** — compute instances managed by Auto Scaling groups for automated capacity management

---

## What I Demonstrated

Through this SimuLearn, I demonstrated knowledge of:

- Auto-healing capabilities of Auto Scaling groups including instance health checks and automatic replacement
- Automatic scaling mechanisms for responding to changing application demand
- Auto Scaling group configuration including minimum, maximum, and desired capacity
- Resource boundary implementation to control scaling costs and capacity
- Scaling policies and their configuration for metric-based scaling decisions
- Time-based scaling strategies for predictable demand patterns
- CloudWatch metrics and alarms for monitoring application performance
- Launch templates and instance configuration within Auto Scaling groups
- High availability design through multi-AZ Auto Scaling deployments

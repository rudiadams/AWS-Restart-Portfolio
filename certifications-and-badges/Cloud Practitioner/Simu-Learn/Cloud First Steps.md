# Cloud First Steps

## Overview
My exploration of AWS Global Infrastructure and the principles of high availability — analyzing the benefits of AWS's distributed global architecture, comparing Regions and Availability Zones, and implementing resilient solutions across multiple Availability Zones using Amazon EC2.

---

## Services I Worked With

- **Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud (EC2)** — scalable virtual server infrastructure deployed across multiple Availability Zones to ensure high availability and fault tolerance

---

## What I Demonstrated

Through this SimuLearn, I demonstrated knowledge of:

- The strategic benefits of AWS's Global Infrastructure for reliability and performance
- The architectural differences between AWS Regions and Availability Zones
- How to leverage multiple Availability Zones to design highly available solutions
- EC2 instance deployment across distributed infrastructure for redundancy and fault tolerance
- The relationship between global infrastructure design and application resilience
